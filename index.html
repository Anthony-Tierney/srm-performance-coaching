My Services

1-2-1 Coaching

Dedicated personal training sessions tailored to your specific goals, fitness level, and preferences. Full attention and expert guidance every step of the way.

2-1-2 Coaching

Train with a friend or partner and benefit from shared motivation and accountability. Receive a 25% discount when you train with a partner or friend during your session. Perfect for couples, friends, or colleagues wanting to achieve fitness goals together.

Boxing / Kickboxing

Specialized combat sports training combining boxing and kickboxing techniques. Build explosive power, precision, and confidence while getting an intense cardiovascular workout. Perfect for fitness goals and self-defense skills.

Online Training

Access professional coaching from anywhere. Perfect for those with busy schedules or who prefer training from home. Full support and programming included.

Nutrition Planning

Customized nutrition strategies to complement your training and maximize results. Simple, sustainable approaches that fit your lifestyle.

Group Kickboxing Classes

High-energy group sessions combining kickboxing drills and cardio conditioning. Train alongside others, build community, and push your limits with the motivation and energy of a group. Perfect for both beginners and experienced fighters.

8-Week Training Plans

Intensive, results-driven programs tailored over 8 weeks combining expert training guidance, nutrition support, and accountability. Designed to deliver visible progress and sustainable habit-building for lasting transformation.
